# Shared support policy

WAJetsamFix is independently developed, distributed voluntarily and provided **as-is**.

No edition includes guaranteed individual support, response time, maintenance schedule, release cadence, future release or universal compatibility. Publishing a package does not create an ongoing service obligation.

## Technical readiness and publication

A private Alpha, completed milestone or future RC may remain private even after successful testing. Public publication is a separate maintainer decision based on technical quality, expected support burden, available personal and family time, infrastructure costs and long-term sustainability.

A roadmap entry does not create a release promise.

## Sustainable development

Research, private testing, build infrastructure, device validation, electricity, documentation and compatibility work require substantial personal time and resources.

The current public RootHide Edition 0.7.1 package remains available. Future editions and major features will be published only when they are technically ready and sustainable for the project.

See the [project sustainability policy](SUSTAINABILITY.md).

## Sponsorship

Voluntary sponsorship helps offset the work and costs required for continued public development. It does not purchase guaranteed support, private-build access, a specific feature, compatibility with an individual setup, influence over safety decisions or a release date.

There is no sponsor threshold or pay-to-release promise.

## Reports

Reports may be reviewed at the maintainer's discretion. A useful report identifies:

- the exact WAJetsamFix edition and package version;
- device, iOS and jailbreak environment;
- WhatsApp and relevant injection-framework versions;
- selected memory profile when applicable;
- complete output from the documented edition diagnostic command.

Private Alpha availability or a public roadmap entry must not be interpreted as a support or release commitment.
