# Voluntary sponsorships

The current public WAJetsamFix RootHide Edition 0.7.1 package remains freely available under its documented **as-is** terms.

Future editions, major features and compatibility work are independently developed in the maintainer's personal time. That work includes reverse engineering, private device testing, jailbreak-specific integration, build infrastructure, package auditing, diagnostics, documentation and long-term review as WhatsApp and jailbreak environments change.

It also consumes time otherwise reserved for family, rest and paid responsibilities, while creating direct infrastructure, device-testing and electricity costs.

Voluntary sponsorship helps make continued public development more viable and helps offset the work required to turn private engineering into reviewed public releases.

## Sponsorship does not purchase

Sponsorship does **not**:

- purchase guaranteed or priority support;
- create a service obligation;
- purchase access to private builds;
- guarantee compatibility with an individual setup;
- purchase a specific feature;
- determine technical or safety decisions;
- guarantee a release or release date.

There is no sponsor threshold, countdown or pay-to-release promise.

A private Alpha, completed milestone or RC may remain private. Technical readiness and public publication are separate maintainer decisions.

## Publication model

The project follows this model:

> Keep development private and publish only when it is technically ready and sustainable.

The current stable package remains available. Future work may be published, postponed, limited to documentation or retained privately depending on technical quality, support impact, personal capacity and sustainability.

See the [project sustainability policy](SUSTAINABILITY.md).
