# Support policy

WAJetsamFix is a personal project distributed voluntarily and provided **as-is**.

- No individual or immediate technical support is guaranteed.
- No response time is promised.
- Compatibility with every device, iOS version, WhatsApp release, tweak combination or jailbreak configuration is not guaranteed.
- No fixed maintenance schedule or continuous development is promised.
- Installation and use remain the user's responsibility.

The absence of guaranteed support does not reduce the care taken in development, testing, packaging and documentation. It only makes clear that distributing the tweak does not create an ongoing service obligation.

## Future updates

Updates are expected to be infrequent. A future WhatsApp, iOS, Dopamine, RootHide or ElleKit change may require reviewed compatibility work, but WAJetsamFix will never guess or silently broaden its targeting policy.

## Before reporting a problem

Use the instructions for the installed edition and provide the edition name, package version, device, iOS version, jailbreak version, WhatsApp version and complete output of the edition's status command when one is available.

Review diagnostic output before sharing it publicly and remove any information you consider private.
