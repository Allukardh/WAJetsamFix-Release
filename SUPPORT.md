# Support policy

WAJetsamFix is a personal project distributed voluntarily and provided **as-is**.

## What is not guaranteed

- No individual, immediate or private technical support is guaranteed.
- No response time or service level is promised.
- Compatibility with every device, iOS version, WhatsApp release, tweak combination or jailbreak configuration is not guaranteed.
- No fixed maintenance schedule, continuous development, RC or future release is promised.
- Installation, configuration and use remain the user's responsibility.

Publishing a free package does not create an ongoing service obligation, transfer ownership of the maintainer's future time to the community or require private development to become public.

## Technical readiness is not a publication commitment

A private build may be technically functional, field-tested and close to release quality while remaining private.

A public release is a separate maintainer decision based on:

- technical safety and quality;
- expected support burden;
- available personal and family time;
- infrastructure, device-testing and electricity costs;
- the effort required to maintain compatibility after publication;
- whether continued public development is sustainable.

The maintainer may pause work, keep an Alpha or RC private indefinitely, publish documentation without a binary or decide not to release a planned feature.

## Development and sustainability

Researching WhatsApp behavior, validating jailbreak-specific integration, maintaining build infrastructure, reviewing diagnostics and producing user-facing documentation require substantial personal time and resources.

This work competes with family, rest and paid responsibilities. The current public RootHide Edition 0.7.1 package remains available, but everything beyond it is additional work rather than an owed continuation.

Future editions, major features and compatibility updates will be considered only when they are technically justified and sustainable for the project. A feature being demonstrated privately does not create a release promise or deadline.

See the [project sustainability policy](docs/shared/SUSTAINABILITY.md).

## Sponsorship

Voluntary sponsorship helps offset development time, build infrastructure, device testing, electricity and the work required to turn private engineering into reviewed public releases.

Sponsorship does **not** purchase:

- guaranteed support or response priority;
- private troubleshooting;
- access to private builds;
- a specific feature;
- compatibility with a particular setup;
- influence over technical or safety decisions;
- a release or release date.

There is no sponsor threshold or pay-to-release promise.

## Future updates

Updates are expected to be infrequent. A future WhatsApp, iOS, Dopamine, RootHide, RootHide Bootstrap or ElleKit change may require reviewed compatibility work, but WAJetsamFix will never guess or silently broaden its targeting policy.

The maintainer may keep experimental builds private until their technical quality, support impact and long-term sustainability are understood.

## Before reporting a problem

Use the instructions for the installed edition and provide:

- edition name;
- package version;
- device and iOS version;
- jailbreak and injection-framework versions;
- WhatsApp version;
- selected memory profile when applicable;
- complete output of the edition's documented status command.

Review diagnostic output before sharing it publicly and remove any information you consider private. Reports may be reviewed at the maintainer's discretion.
